About Us

Your Partner For Clinical Trial


ResearchMentor was founded in 2016, launched a public research marketplace. Over the 3 years, ResearchMentor has worked on clinical projects for researchers in Korea company. To date, we’ve established partnerships with INDIA and created Bridge CRO. Bridge CRO is a ResearchMentor’s platform brand that allows Korean pharmaceutical companies and India laboratory to cooperate with each other to enable clinical trials to proceed. One-Stop Service is provided to our researcher from setup of pre-clinical test to Data Analysis. Because of these service, we help researchers to focus their attention on carrying out their project. Bridge CRO enable researchers to saving time, reducing costs, ensuring compliance.

What We Do ?

Initiation Of Korea-India Coexistence In Clinical Trials


India has a good environment for clinical trials, so it is suitable to solve various problems of Korean companies. BridgeCRO allows Korean pharmaceutical companies(Sponsor) and India laboratory(CRO) to cooperate with each other to enable clinical trials to proceed



Strength


The existing procedure required about 60 days to start the clinical trial, but it took about 10 days to start using BridgeCRO.



Benefit


Bridge CRO is simplifies how companies do business with each other. And as a result, a variety of benefits are created.


  • Sponsor can request various agencies online at once and receive various quotations and proposals easily and conveniently.
  • Sponsor can select the most efficient testing institute with a pre-set budget. ​
  • BridgeCRO allows the sponsor to identify and manage all aspects of the clinical trials.
  • BridgeCRO billing consolidation eliminates the need for finance to negotiate payment terms, manage invoices and payments and onboard new suppliers.
  • India is rich with a large number of regulatory affair specialists, making it easy to create high-level reports with a high level of understanding of international standards and specifications
